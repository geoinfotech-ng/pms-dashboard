Key Events Affecting Nigerian Fuel Prices

How Global Oil Prices Affect Nigerian Petrol Prices

Nigeria is Africa's largest oil producer yet has historically imported most refined petroleum. When Brent crude rises, import costs increase proportionally. The pump price is shaped by the crude oil benchmark, the NGN/USD exchange rate, limited domestic refining capacity, transportation costs (northern states pay more), and government policy — particularly the 2023 subsidy removal which exposed prices to market forces.

About This Dashboard

Fuel prices are sourced from the National Bureau of Statistics PMS Price Watch reports, updated monthly via a published Google Sheet. Brent crude prices come from the Alpha Vantage API (EIA data).
