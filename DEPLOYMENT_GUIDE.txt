DEPLOYMENT GUIDE — Nigeria PMS Price Dashboard v2
==================================================

CHANGES IN v2:
- Redesigned UI with editorial/data-journalism aesthetic
- Dark mode toggle (saves preference to localStorage)
- Pie chart now shows PMS price distribution by price band
- Choropleth map with proper legend (min/avg/max labels)
- Key events updated with US-Iran war (Feb 2026) and oil price surge

STRUCTURE:

  js/config.js        — Configuration, events, settings
  js/fuelData.js      — Data fetching from Google Sheet
  js/map.js           — Leaflet choropleth map

  js/ui.js            — UI logic, dark mode, PDF generation
  js/logo.js          — Logo base64
  assets/logo.png     — Logo image

DEPENDENCIES (loaded via CDN):

  - jsPDF 2.5.2
  - Google Fonts: Playfair Display, IBM Plex Sans, DM Mono

TO DEPLOY:
